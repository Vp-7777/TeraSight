# TeraSight — Brand Guidelines

## Product Name

# TeraSight

---

## Official Tagline

**Turning Environmental Imagery into Actionable Intelligence.**

---

## Brand Motto

**See. Understand. Restore.**

---

## Powered By

**Powered by PrithviQ AI — Advanced Environmental Computer Vision Engine.**

PrithviQ AI is the dedicated machine learning engine responsible for environmental waste detection and classification. TeraSight is the platform, analytics, and decision-support layer built on top of this engine.

---

# Brand Personality

TeraSight should feel:

- Intelligent.
- Trustworthy.
- Scientific.
- Modern.
- Calm.
- Optimistic.
- Mission-driven.
- Premium without being flashy.

The product should inspire confidence in governments, NGOs, researchers, and environmental organizations.

---

# Design Philosophy

The interface should prioritize:
- Clarity over decoration.
- Data over gimmicks.
- Smooth interactions.
- Clean layouts.
- Professional dashboards.

Avoid overly playful or cartoon-like elements.

The overall experience should feel like a combination of:
- Linear,
- Notion,
- Arc Browser,
- Apple Human Interface principles,
- and modern enterprise SaaS dashboards.

---

# Visual Language

The UI should communicate:
- Environmental intelligence.
- Precision.
- Transparency.
- Actionability.
- Sustainability.

Images and graphics should emphasize:
- Earth observation.
- Water bodies.
- Drone imagery.
- AI analytics.
- Environmental restoration.

---

# Color Philosophy

Primary themes:
- Deep Blue → Water & Trust.
- Forest Green → Sustainability & Restoration.
- Soft White / Light Gray → Clarity & Simplicity.
- Dark Slate → Professional dashboards.

Accent colors should be subtle and used primarily for:
- Alerts.
- Risk indicators.
- Active selections.

Avoid oversaturated palettes.

---

# Typography

Preferred characteristics:
- Modern sans-serif.
- High readability.
- Clean spacing.
- Suitable for dashboards and reports.

The interface should feel premium and accessible rather than decorative.

---

# UI Principles

## Landing Page
- Strong hero section.
- Large mission statement.
- Interactive environmental imagery.
- Minimal but elegant animations.

## Dashboard
- Card-based layout.
- Information hierarchy.
- Clear metrics and charts.
- Fast navigation.

## Analysis View
- Large annotated image preview.
- Waste statistics.
- AI recommendations.
- Downloadable reports.

---

# AI Generation Rules

When generating UI or frontend code:
- Prefer elegant and minimal layouts.
- Avoid generic Bootstrap appearance.
- Use modern SaaS design patterns.
- Maintain consistency across pages.
- Design for NGOs, municipalities, and environmental analysts.

---

# Brand Story

TeraSight combines the ideas of:
- "Terra" (Earth),
- and the familiar expression "Tera" (Your),

symbolizing a platform that empowers **your vision for a cleaner planet**.

TeraSight transforms ordinary environmental images into meaningful intelligence that helps people understand, protect, and restore ecosystems.

TeraSight is more than a software platform.

It is an AI-native environmental intelligence ecosystem built to help humanity better understand and restore the world around us.
